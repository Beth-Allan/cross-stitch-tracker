# Bulk Supply Editor

**What:** A dedicated "Manage Supplies" page for bulk-editing supply quantities, adding multiple supplies at once, and managing project-supply links in batch.

**From DesignOS:** `product-plan/sections/supply-tracking-and-shopping/bulk-supply-editor.png` and `BulkSupplyEditor.tsx`

**Design shows:**
- "Back to project" header with supply count + edit toggle
- Collapsible Thread/Beads/Specialty sections
- Inline search-to-add per section
- Editable Required/Acquired columns
- Bulk operations

**Why post-MVP:** One-at-a-time supply management works for MVP. Bulk editing becomes valuable when kitting a large project with 50+ thread colors — that's when the single-add flow gets tedious.
